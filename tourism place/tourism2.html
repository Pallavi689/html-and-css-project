Madhya pradesh

Travels In The Heart Of   India

Madhya Pradesh is the second largest Indian state by area and
the fifth largest state by population with over 72 million
residents.Its capital is Bhopal, and the largest city is
Indore, with Satna, Jabalpur, Gwalior, Ujjain, Sagar being the
other major cities.Tourism in Madhya Pradesh has been an attraction
of India because of its location in the centre of the country. Madhya
Pradesh has won Best Tourism State National award for 3 consecutive
years i.e. 2017, 2016 and 2015
